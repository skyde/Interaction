Here is a list of shortcuts for Visual Studio.

It's hard to export the settings directly, but somewhat quick to reassign them so just do that.

<Shift+F2> Debug.Start
<Shift+F3> ReSharper.ReSharper_GotoFile

!(needs to move 16 lines) <Shift+F4> Edit.ScrollLineUp
!(needs to move 16 lines) <Shift+F6> Edit.ScrollLineDown

<Shift+F7> Build.Cancel

!(need a way for this to be context dependent) <Shift+F7> Debug.StopDebugging

<Shift+F8> Edit.GoToDefinition
<Shift+F11> Edit.ToggleLineComment
<Ctrl+Shift+F> FastFind
<Alt+Left Arrow> ReSharper.ReSharper_NavigateBackward
<Alt+Right Arrow> ReSharper.ReSharper_NavigateForward
